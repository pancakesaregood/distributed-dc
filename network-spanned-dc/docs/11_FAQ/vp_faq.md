# VP FAQ

## What is the governance model?
- Architecture and implementation are governed by documented acceptance criteria, change review, and decision tracking.

## How is delivery phased to limit risk?
- Foundation per site first, then inter-site activation, then service onboarding, followed by resilience proof and handover.

## Where are budget controls applied?
- Open-source-first tooling, staged site rollout, and routed-access baseline before advanced SDN options.

## What are the key go/no-go gates?
- Readiness gate, per-site baseline validation, inter-site routing validation, and DR evidence gate.

## What operating model is expected post-go-live?
- GitOps-driven change control, centralized observability, runbook-based incident response, and scheduled lifecycle management.
