# User FAQ

## Will this change how I access services?
- Most users should see no workflow change; access continues through approved internal and remote paths.

## Will there be planned downtime?
- Some maintenance windows are expected during rollout, with advance communication and staged cutovers.

## How is remote access handled?
- Through VPN and policy-based access controls with MFA requirements.

## Will my data stay available if one site fails?
- Critical services are designed for multi-site continuity based on service tier and replication model.

## Who do I contact if something is not working?
- Contact the service desk first; they follow documented triage and escalation paths.
