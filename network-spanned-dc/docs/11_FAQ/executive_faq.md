# Executive FAQ

## What problem does this program solve?
- It reduces single-site risk, improves service recoverability, and standardizes operations across all sites.

## Why this architecture approach?
- It balances resilience and cost by using Layer 3 multi-site design, open-source-first tooling, and phased delivery.

## How is business risk managed?
- Through explicit failure-domain design, DR runbooks, backup validation, and measurable acceptance criteria.

## How will success be measured?
- By meeting documented RTO/RPO targets, passing DR/restore exercises, and achieving operations handover readiness.

## What are the major program dependencies?
- WAN capability alignment, platform decisions in the appendix register, and cross-team delivery discipline.
