# Business Case 02 - Security Control Uplift

## Decision Statement
Invest in policy-enforced segmentation, MFA-first access, and centralized security telemetry to lower breach probability and blast radius.

## Current Problem
- Inconsistent control enforcement can allow lateral movement.
- Limited unified visibility increases detection and response time.

## Proposed Investment
- Standardize firewall zone policy with default deny.
- Enforce MFA for privileged and remote access paths.
- Centralize firewall, WAF, auth, and host logs for investigation.

## Cost Drivers
- Security platform engineering and policy hardening effort.
- Logging and retention infrastructure.
- Operational training and runbook integration.

## Expected Business Outcomes
- Reduced security incident frequency and severity.
- Improved audit readiness and evidence quality.
- Faster incident containment and recovery.

## KPIs
- MFA adoption rate for privileged access.
- Mean time to detect and mean time to contain incidents.
- Number of high-severity findings related to segmentation/access controls.

## Recommendation
Approve as a risk-reduction program with measurable security and governance returns.
