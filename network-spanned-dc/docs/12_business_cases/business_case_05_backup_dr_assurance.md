# Business Case 05 - Backup and DR Assurance

## Decision Statement
Fund backup immutability and DR validation as a formal assurance program to reduce financial exposure from ransomware and major outages.

## Current Problem
- Backup success does not guarantee restore success without regular testing.
- Recovery confidence is low when DR evidence is stale or incomplete.

## Proposed Investment
- Implement 3-2-1-aligned retention with immutable or off-domain copy.
- Execute scheduled restore drills and scenario-based DR testing.
- Establish evidence package for RTO/RPO attainment and exception tracking.

## Cost Drivers
- Backup storage tiers and retention footprint.
- DR exercise labor and test environment overhead.
- Monitoring and reporting integration.

## Expected Business Outcomes
- Higher confidence in recoverability under stress events.
- Reduced downtime and data-loss exposure.
- Better legal, audit, and executive defensibility.

## KPIs
- Restore success rate by data class.
- Percent of Tier 1 systems with current DR evidence.
- Measured RTO/RPO compliance rate.

## Recommendation
Approve as a resilience assurance program with direct risk and continuity value.
